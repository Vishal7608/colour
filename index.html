Roof, Interior, and Exterior Painting Work for Factories and Warehouses

We add new value to corporate facilities with functional painting.
We offer a one-stop solution to painting challenges.
Painting damage caused by aging not only significantly degrades the durability of the building, but also leads to a deteriorating work environment and negatively impacts employee motivation. Uchigen's painting work for factories and warehouses is a total solution that combines our abundant on-site experience, paint knowledge, and highly refined application technology.

Functional painting is an important choice that not only preserves the aesthetics of the building, but also protects the future of employees and communities

Although architectural painting tends to be focused primarily on appearance, its functions are diverse and go beyond mere aesthetics.
In particular, in recent years, with the advent of paints and painting methods that have various features, attention has been focused on "functional paint and painting" that adds new value to buildings and on-site environments.
Uchigen uses its extensive product network as a paint trading company, work site knowledge backed by experience, and refined application technology to provide painting solutions that are ideal for industrial facilities such as factories and warehouses.

Uchigen’s corporate painting solutions that offer the value you want realize and solve your current challenges

Achieve energy saving and environmental improvements

  • Suppress temperature rise with heat-shielding paints and heat-insulating paints
  • Control the amount of sunlight with window heat shield painting and window film
  • Ensure employee health with sntibacterial and antiviral paints
  • Floor painting that can withstand forklifts and AGVs

Extend the life of your facility

  • Roof and exterior wall painting to prevent deterioration of the frame of a building
  • A building that withstands wind and rain through waterproofing work

Achieve long-term maintenance cost savings

  • Utilize inorganic paints and high-durability sealing to reduce life cycle costs

Service Flow

STEP 01 Customers consulting with us

Please let us know about any painting issues or requests for environmental improvement.

STEP 02 On-site survey

We investigate the local situation in detail and identify on-site challenges with the eyes of professional.

STEP 03 Renovation specification proposals

We compare our customers' requests with the results of our on-site survey to select the appropriate surface treatment method and paint. We then offer an optimal renovation plan with a view to total and maintenance costs.

STEP 04 Estimates

We prepare cost estimates for various plans. Uchigen has an integrated system from paint sales to construction, so we can provide accurate estimates, including specifications and number of applications of paint.

STEP 05 Contracts

STEP 06 Color plan proposals

Uchigen's strength is that we take into account various factors such as affinity with the surrounding environment for exterior wall and roof painting, and psychological security for workers in the case of factory and warehouse floors. We use our characteristics as a specialist trading company with our own paint factories to provide detailed support for mixing company colors and the like.

STEP 07 Construction

Painting work is done by skilled engineers under thorough safety and quality control. Uchigen is also responsible for construction supervision, which ensures high construction quality.

STEP 08 Inspection and hand over

The final inspection and delivery will be carried out in the presence of the customer and Uchigen's construction supervisor.

STEP 09 After-sales follow-up

Even after the delivery, we will visit for follow-up any time when customers contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions

I am looking to renovate a roof and exterior walls. I need an estimate for budgeting purposes. What should I do?

First of all, give us a call or send an email. The process will be smooth if you tell us, "I would like a cost estimate for construction work (painting and waterproofing)." Our sales team will be happy to assist you. No outside parties will be involved. We offer estimates and diagnoses free of charge.

I don't know the proper painting specifications for the renovation work.

If you have any problems with the construction details, please contact us. Renovation specifications vary greatly, so we will propose the appropriate specifications while incorporating customer requirements.

What is the largest scale of construction work that you can handle?

We have obtained a special construction business permit, so there is no limit to the size of painting work we can do. Please leave even large-scale repair work to us.

Do you have any paints that are effective for saving electricity?

Recently, the demand for heat-insulating and heat-shielding paints has been increasing. It is especially effective in saving electricity used for air conditioning and other applications.
We are increasingly aware of the importance of environmental considerations, as expressed in terms of carbon neutrality and SDGs. Painting buildings does not contribute much to the environment, but by painting with heat-insulating or heat-shielding paints, air conditioning and heating efficiency can be improved, so one can expect to reduce electricity usage.

My office space is too hot. Can you add heat shielding to windows?

Please come to us to discuss sun shield for glass.
In addition to heat shield painting for window glass, we can also apply a heat shielding film.

I'm planning to renovate my exterior walls and roof. Can I get an estimate for things other than painting?

Please contact us for matters other than painting (sealing, waterproofing, cover construction methods, interiors, various repairs, etc.).

I have already received an estimate for the renovation work, but can you provide me with another estimate as a comparison?

We are happy to give you an estimate for comparison.
