Floor Painting Work for Factories and Warehouses

Painting solves problems such as tracks and oil stains left by forklifts and AGVs.
Uchigen's floor painting work for factories and warehouses.
Floor problems in factories and warehouses. Uchigen responds to a variety of problems that vary by the type of industry and business with a wealth of paint knowledge and various application technologies.

We propose and implement floor painting work that is optimal for use applications and building environments in one stop.

The floors of factories and warehouses accumulate various damage every day. Cracked floors and the dust that this generates not only undermine workplace safety, but can also have a variety of negative consequences, such as damaging the company’s image and reducing the motivation of the people who work there.
Uchigen's floor painting work is a painting solution that revives floor surfaces by applying the most suitable paint using the correct method, with the aim of eliminating various problems such as those mentioned above. Based on our knowledge as a specialist paint trading company, we select paints that are optimal for different needs depending on the type of industry and business. By utilizing painting techniques that make the most of the paint's properties, we can create beautiful floor surfaces that are both highly durable and safe.

We resolve the following concerns and requests for factory and warehouse floors with floor painting

  • Uneven ground and steps are obstructing the passage of carts and other vehicles.
  • We need to suppress the contamination caused by chips of peeling paint film.
  • Forklifts and AGVs have left tracks in the floor.
  • I want a floor that can withstand chemicals such as acids and alkalis.
  • The floor paint breaks when tools and other objects fall on it.
  • I want the floor to have antibacterial properties.
  • The floor is damaged by oil, hot water, steam, etc.
  • I want to reduce the number of renovations and reduce life cycle costs.
  • The floor is slippery and dangerous.

Uchigen's 2 Strengths

01
STRONG POINT

For manufacturing, food, pharmaceuticals, medical facilities, etc.
We propose a variety of floor painting according to the type of industry and business

Factory and warehouse floors require an enormous variety of specifications and functions, as frequency of use and loads vary greatly from customer to customer, as well as the substances that cause it to get dirty and the way the facilities are used. Uchigen offers a diverse lineup of products and construction methods so that we can make optimal proposals to meet such customer requests, including cost considerations.

Uchigen’s diverse lineup of products and construction methods

Water-based rigid urethane mortar method
This is an ideal method for use in food factories, kitchens, and the like, where water and oil are used in large amounts, and where there is a severe environment in terms of odor generation and contamination of paint film pieces, etc. It is more durable than epoxy resin and may be applied to AGV courses that are prone to forming tracks

Water-based rigid polyurethane pouring method
Compared to the water-based rigid urethane mortar construction method, the surface is smoother (glossy), so it is often used in food factories because it is easier to clean. The product is resistant to stains and maintains its aesthetic for a long time.

Epoxy resin mortar pouring method
It offers excellent impact resistance, ideal for aisles where forklifts and AGVs travel. Due to the thick paint film, it can be finished smoothly even if there are some uneven spots on the floor surface before painting. It is also resistant to oil, humidity, and water, and boasts high durability.

Epoxy resin pouring paste method
This method is suitable for places where pressure resistance is not required as much as roadways, such as sidewalks. While maintaining the high surface hardness and excellent oil, water, and chemical resistance of epoxy resin, it is possible to reduce construction costs and shorten construction periods.

Coating method (acrylic, urethane, epoxy)
It is a thin film painting that is applied with a roller. Unlike the pouring method, it has little effect on smoothing out the unevenness of the surface, so it is recommended when the current floor surface is smooth and you want to paint the floor mainly to improve aesthetics. It can also be used for partial construction.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm having trouble with unevenness on the floor. Will it require extensive construction?

Most floor defects, such as tracks and trolley wear, can be solved with paint. Uchigen conducts free on-site inspections, so please feel free to call or email us.

How much does it cost to repaint a 100 m² floor?

Floor painting is complex and there are many different painting methods available. We provide estimates after reviewing the detailed on-site conditions in order to propose the best method of construction for the customer's request and the on-site environment. Please request a free on-site inspection first.

No matter how many times I paint the floor, the paint peels off immediately...

It is difficult to say for sure without knowing the local conditions, but if you choose the right paint and painting method, it is possible for the surface to withstand the passage of heavy vehicles such as forklifts.

I would like you to complete the work while we are closed on Saturday and Sunday. Can you do this?

We also do work on Saturday and Sunday. Uchigen has methods that allow us to complete painting in two days, so you can walk on the floor after the weekend is over.

Can you repair only the damaged areas?

Yes, we can. Please feel free to contact us regardless of the area that you need repaired.

I want to have my employees paint the floor. Can you recommend paint for us to use?

We also sell paint only. We will provide you with paints that are suitable for your needs and the current environment, so it will be best if you provide us with detailed information about your current conditions.
However, it is true that the finish quality and durability of the floor painting depend greatly on the application technology. Please consider our professional application as well.
