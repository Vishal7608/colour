Painting Work for Houses, Apartments, and Condominiums

For a long-lasting home that is always beautiful.
Uchigen's Painting Work for Houses, Apartments, and Condominiums.
A variety of paints are used in houses, apartments, and condominiums to protect the buildings from wind and rain. Uchigen offers a full range of "house painting" services, including painting of roofs and exterior walls, as well as waterproofing and sealing of rooftops and verandas, to keep buildings beautiful and extend their lifespan.

We propose painting that not only increases the asset value of the building, but also provides comfort and energy savings

The paint film is less than 1 mm thick, protecting the homes that are constantly exposed to harsh environments such as wind, rain, and ultraviolet light. This paint film endures heat and ultraviolet light to prevent damage to the building itself. Therefore, if you do not repaint within a certain period of time, the paint film will eventually deteriorate, and damage from wind, rain, and sunlight will be transmitted directly to the building itself. This is a more serious problem than it appears, as it will greatly affect the lifespan of the building and will have a direct and negative impact on daily life, such as allowing rainwater to get inside the building.
The life expectancy of Japanese houses is said to be 60 or more years, but that long lifespan is only attainted with regular roof and exterior wall repainting. Uchigen offers the best painting solutions for detached houses, apartments, and condominiums, taking into consideration not only the aesthetics of the building, but also comfort, durability, and cost, and proposes plans that will satisfy each customer.

Residential repainting proposals to reduce lifetime costs

Roof and exterior wall paint protects buildings from ultraviolet light, wind and rain by sacrificing itself. As a result, if the paint film deteriorates and the building itself is damaged, you will incur repair costs above that required for mere repainting. Repainting before that happens can reduce the total maintenance cost. At Uchigen, we offer proposals that look 20 years into the future, using "inorganic paints" that surpass fluorine-based paints and highly durable, weather-resistant sealants.

What is the standard to require repainting?

Here we explain what kind of conditions necessitate repainting. The following are symptoms that require maintenance in the main areas:

01Color fading and chalking

Symptom

You can see the chalking (whitening) of the paint film. The paint film has deteriorated and is losing its protective function for the base material.

Cause

This is because ultraviolet light, temperatures, water and the like acted for a long time, causing the resin and pigments on the paint film surface to deteriorate, and the paint film surface gradually depleted into powder.

Solution

It is necessary to restore the protection and aesthetics of the base material by repainting. The key is to select paints that are weather resistant.

02Cracks

Symptom

You can see the cracks. If left unattended, atmospheric carbon dioxide and water infiltration will accelerate the neutralization of concrete and cause water leakage.

Cause

This is caused by external forces applied to the building, or volumetric shrinkage caused by differences in temperature and humidity when the concrete dries or due to differences in season.

Solution

We repair cracks larger than 0.3 mm by low-pressure epoxy resin injection or U-cut processing, and repair those smaller than 0.3 mm by rubbing in filler or elastic sealant.

03Peeling of the paint film

Symptom

The adhesion of the paint film between the paint layer and the steel surface has weakened, causing some of the paint film to peel off.

Cause

In paint film with poor water and alkali resistance, metallic soap forms between the zinc layer and the rust-preventive layer, reducing adhesion.

Solution

We completely remove weak paint film and paint film with poor adhesion. We select specifications with excellent adhesion.

04Rust

Symptom

Rust can be seen everywhere in the iron parts.

Cause

Iron reacts with moisture and oxygen in the air to form iron oxide.

Solution

Since the rust prevention performance of the paint film is largely dependent on the degree of rust removal of the base, it is necessary to paint only after sufficiently scraping the surface clean.

05Algae, mold, moss

Symptom

The appearance is marred by the growth of algae and moss.

Cause

The growth conditions of algae and mold occur in the pH neutral range and in moderate sunlight, temperature (25-30° C) and humidity conditions.

Solution

We remove algae, mold, and moss and paints with high-pressure water washing. If the mold is severe, we may use chemicals for disinfection. It is also important to select paints that have anti-algae and anti-mold effects.

06Sealing degradation

Symptom

There is a decrease in the elasticity that should normally be present and breakage. This may cause water leakage.

Cause

This is caused by aging deterioration due to ultraviolet rays, heat, oxidation, rainwater, etc.

Solution

Sealants play a critical role in maintaining the airtightness and watertightness of buildings. This assumes that the existing sealant will be removed and replaced.

Service Flow

STEP 01 Customers consulting with us

We look forward to hearing from owners of detached houses, apartments, and condominiums. Please let us know about any issues you may have regarding painting your home or requests for environmental improvement.

STEP 02 Building diagnostics

Our staff will visit your site and check the current condition, including the building's frame, exterior, and common areas such as hallways in the case of an apartment or condominium. They conduct a survey and diagnosis of buildings and architectural systems through visual inspection, palpation, and percussion. Based on the results of this survey, they will be able to rationally decide the optimal "when," "to what extent," and "how" to carry out the work.

STEP 03 Renovation specification proposals

We compare our customers' requests with the results of our on-site survey to select the appropriate surface treatment method and paint. We then offer an optimal renovation plan with a view to total and maintenance costs.

STEP 04 Estimates

We make estimates based on the requests and diagnostic results. Essentially, we submit multiple plans, each with its own merits, and explain them as we present them. Uchigen is also an integrated system from paint sales to construction, so the accuracy of estimation is one of the features. This prevents problems before they happen.

STEP 05 Contracts

Once you are satisfied with the specifications and scope of work, a contract will be made. *We can also assist with consultations regarding various renovation loans.

STEP 06 Meeting about construction period and color

We will decide the construction period in consultation with the customer. In addition, during meetings about colors, we can perform color simulations using photos to check the paint colors and paint divisions.

STEP 07 Work announcement to neighbors

Construction involves making noise or using water. Uchigen will carefully explain the work content and schedule to neighbors to ensure the construction is completed smoothly.

STEP 08 Construction

Install a temporary scaffold and cover the entire building with a protective screen (mesh sheet). After that, we will proceed with high-pressure washing, sealing work, painting work, and waterproofing work. Uchigen personnel will supervise the site and proceed with construction work in safety first.

STEP 09 Inspection and hand over

Once construction is complete, the Uchigen site manager will first perform a quality check and do rework as necessary. After that, if there is a point pointed out by the customer, we will repair it, confirm that there is no problem, and hand it over.

STEP 10 After-sales follow-up

Even after the delivery, we will visit for follow-up any time when customers contact us.

Should I get estimates from multiple companies?

We would like to say that simply contacting us would be sufficient, but it is common for people to consider two or three companies at a time. It is important to make a comprehensive judgment not only on the price, but also on the quality of the paint and the application technologies.

Because the structure and construction of the building can vary greatly, we cannot give a price estimate based on the area alone.
